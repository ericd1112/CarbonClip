CarbonClip

Based upon research at Cambridge University, the CarbonClip (patent pending) is a carbon fiber clip angle, for attaching curtain wall framing to a building's structure, while eliminating the thermal shorts that come with traditional metal clip angles. Available for use and manufacture under license.
